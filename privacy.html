Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Your privacy is our priority. Nummira is designed as an offline-first application, meaning your financial data is stored locally on your device by default.

1. Data Storage

All your transaction history, asset details, and personal goals are stored in a local encrypted database on your smartphone. We do not transmit this data to our servers for storage.

2. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage

Nummira uses third-party AI providers to offer features like asset valuation, risk analysis, and forecasting. We partner with the following providers:

  • Gemini (Google)
  • OpenAI (GPT-4)
  • Perplexity

3. Data Anonymization

When you use AI features, strict anonymization protocols are applied before any data leaves your device:

  • All personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, addresses, and exact bank account numbers are removed.
  • Specific asset names may be generalized (e.g., "1 Bedroom Apartment in Miami" -> "Residential Property in US South").
  • We only send the mathematical vectors and sanitized context required for the specific query.

4. User Responsibility

While we strive to provide accurate insights, AI models can occasionally produce incorrect or "hallucinated" information. You should verify all valuations and financial advice independently.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact privacy@nummira.com.
